# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Veltiosoft Inc. ("Veltiosoft", "we", "us") handles information in connection with Cordierite, our local-first dictation app for macOS, and this website at cordierite.veltiosoft.com (together, the "Service").

## Operator

Veltiosoft Inc.  
Website: [veltiosoft.com](https://veltiosoft.com/)  
Email: [contact@veltiosoft.com](mailto:contact@veltiosoft.com)

## Scope

This policy applies to the Cordierite macOS application and the Cordierite product website. It does not cover third-party websites or services that Cordierite may link to.

## On-device processing

Cordierite transcribes speech on your Mac. Audio and transcript data are processed locally using Apple Speech and/or Whisper (whisper.cpp). We do not receive, store, or transmit your voice recordings or transcription results to our servers.

## Data we do not persist in the app

Cordierite is designed not to keep a history of your recordings or transcripts inside the app. When you finish a dictation session, the audio and transcript are not saved as app content for later review.

## Local storage on your Mac

Cordierite may store the following data locally in your Mac's Application Support directory:

- App settings such as hotkey, language, and recognition engine preferences
- Whisper model files that you choose to download
- User dictionary entries, when that feature is available

This data remains on your device unless you remove it by deleting the app, its support files, or downloaded models.

## macOS permissions

Cordierite requests macOS permissions only to provide dictation features:

- **Microphone** — to capture your voice for transcription
- **Input Monitoring** — to detect global hotkeys
- **Accessibility** — to paste transcribed text into the app you are using

Permission status is checked locally to guide setup. We do not collect permission status on our servers.

## This website

The Cordierite website is a static product page. We do not currently use analytics, advertising trackers, or similar measurement tools on this site. Standard web server or CDN logs maintained by our hosting provider may include technical information such as IP address, browser type, and requested URL.

## Pro plan (planned)

Cordierite may offer an optional paid tier called Pro in the future. Pro is planned but not available yet. If we introduce subscriptions or other paid features, payment processing may be handled by a third-party provider. In that case, billing-related information would be processed according to that provider's terms and our updated policies. We will revise this page before Pro or other paid features become available.

## Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
